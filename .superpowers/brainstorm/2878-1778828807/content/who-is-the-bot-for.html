Who is the Telegram bot for?

This shapes the entire design — authentication, language, what the bot collects, and what it returns.

A

Clients / Customers

External people (homeowners, contractors) who want a quick quote. They send room dimensions via Telegram, get a calculation result and a CAD drawing back — without ever touching the CRM web app.

Good for

  • Lead generation
  • Self-service quotes
  • No login needed

Needs

  • Public-facing bot
  • No CRM account required
  • Safe sandboxing from CRM data
B

Internal Staff / Salespeople

Your own team (salespeople in the field, on the go) who use Telegram as a mobile-friendly alternative to the web app. Bot creates real projects in the CRM on their behalf.

Good for

  • Field sales speed
  • Real CRM data
  • Proper audit trail

Needs

  • CRM user linked to Telegram
  • Permission checks
C

Both — but start with one

Ultimately both, but they're architecturally different flows. Pick which to build first; the other can be added later without rework.

Good for

  • Full vision

Risk

  • Scope creep
  • Two auth systems
  • More surface area
