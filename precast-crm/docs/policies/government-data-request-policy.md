# Etalon — Government Data Request Policy

**Adopted:** 11 June 2026
**Owner / responsible person:** Azizbek Dadabaev (business owner)
**Scope:** all customer personal data held by Etalon (etalontbm.uz), including data
received through the Meta/Instagram platform (messages, contact details, profile
information) and data collected via Telegram or directly in our CRM.

## Policy

1. **Legality review.** Any request from a public authority for customer data is
   reviewed for legality **before** any response is given. This includes verifying
   the identity of the requesting authority and the legal basis cited for the
   request.
2. **Challenging unlawful requests.** Requests we consider unlawful will be
   challenged or refused.
3. **Data minimization.** If a disclosure is legally required, we disclose only
   the **minimum data necessary** to satisfy the request.
4. **Documentation.** Every request, our response, and the legal reasoning and
   persons involved are documented and retained in our records.
